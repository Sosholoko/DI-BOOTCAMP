-IRON MAN-


Iron Man is a fictional character of the fictional world of Avengers. He's a superhero who's defined as Tony Stark, the super rich CEO of Stark Industries. After a bad time in Afghanistan where he was held by terrorists, he decided to use his mentals capacities and money to build a super armor. He quickly decides to have a little revenge for his bad time and continues to fight bad guys on the account of the US government. He'll be rapidly critisized for his "originals" methods in terms of fighting criminals. Netherless, he'll become a really good asset for the US government againt criminality. He's actually considered of one of the favorites character of the Marvel Universe.
